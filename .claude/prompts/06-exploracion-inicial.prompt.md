I'm designing {{PROJECT_OR_FEATURE}}. Create an agent team to explore this from different angles:

- **UX teammate:** Focus on user experience, flows, and usability
- **Architecture teammate:** Focus on technical architecture and structure
- **Devil's advocate teammate:** Challenge assumptions, identify risks, and stress-test the approach

From there, Claude creates the team with a shared task list, spawns teammates for each perspective, has them explore the problem, synthesizes findings, and attempts to clean up the team when finished.
